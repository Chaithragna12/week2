TIRUMALA

Tirumala Venkateswara Temple

The primary attraction of Tirumala, this temple is dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu. It is one of the most visited religious sites in the world, drawing millions of devotees annually. The temple is known for its elaborate rituals, architecture, and the famous laddu prasadam.

Sri Venkateswara Swamy Vaari Temple

Located near the main temple, this temple is dedicated to Lord Venkateswara. It is a must-visit for those seeking spiritual solace and blessings from the deity.

Silathoranam

This natural rock formation is a geological wonder and is believed to be a naturally formed arch. It holds great significance in Hindu mythology and is a popular spot for tourists.

Sri Varahaswami Temple

Situated on the banks of the Swami Pushkarini Lake, this temple is dedicated to Varaha, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu. It is believed that devotees should visit this temple before entering the main Tirumala temple.

Akasa Ganga

A sacred waterfall near Tirumala, Akasa Ganga is considered holy by the devotees. The water from this waterfall is used for the daily rituals in the temple.

Papavinasam Theertham

This sacred waterfall is believed to cleanse the sins of those who bathe in its waters. It is a serene and scenic spot, attracting many visitors.
